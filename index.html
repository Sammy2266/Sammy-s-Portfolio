Some of my works

  • Naivasha County Refferal Hospital


    Personal Summary
    During my attachment at Naivasha County Referral Hospital, I gained hands-on experience in health information management, hospital data systems, and digital health solutions. Working in the Health Records and IT department, I was actively involved in electronic health records (EHR) management, medical coding, system troubleshooting, and data analysis. This opportunity allowed me to apply my knowledge in a real-world healthcare setting, enhancing my understanding of hospital operations, patient data management, and the role of technology in healthcare delivery.

    Responsibilities:
    - Assisted in digitizing and managing patient records within the hospital’s Health Information System (HIS).
    - Provided technical support and troubleshooting for the hospital’s electronic medical record (EMR) system.
    - Assisted in medical coding (ICD-11) and data classification for patient diagnosis and treatment.
    - Analyzed hospital data trends to generate reports on patient visits, disease patterns, and service efficiency.
    - Worked with IT staff to support digital health initiatives, including telemedicine.
    - Provided basic training and assistance to healthcare staff on EHR system usage.

    Skills Gained:
    - Health Information Management (HIS, EMR, EHR)
    - Data Entry & Medical Coding (ICD-10)
    - Hospital Data Analysis & Reporting
    - System Troubleshooting & IT Support
    - Digital Health & Telemedicine Exposure
    - Teamwork & Collaboration in a Healthcare Setting

    This experience strengthened my ability to handle hospital data efficiently, troubleshoot health systems, and contribute to digital health advancements, reinforcing my passion for health informatics and technology-driven healthcare solutions.
